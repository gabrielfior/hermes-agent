# Competitive Landscape: AI Doctor

## 1. Global Landscape (The Gap)

Most apps are either **transparent** (Apple Health) or **dumb** (Excel trackers). We own the middle: **Private + Intelligent**.

### Personal Health Record (PHR) + AI
| Company | What They Do | Privacy Stance | Gap |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Apple Health** | Aggregates wearables, basic trends | Cloud-synced, limited encryption | No AI treatment planning, no E2EE |
| **Notion/Excel** | Manual logging | Fully local | No AI, no structure, high friction |
| **K Health** | AI symptom checker | Standard encryption | Cloud-only, no long-term memory |
| **Babylon** | AI doctor + triage | Standard HIPAA | No user data ownership |
| **Levels/Whoop** | Wearable + insights | Proprietary cloud | No treatment planning, vendor lock-in |

### AI Medical Assistants
*   **Ada/Buoy:** Symptom checkers. No longitudinal data.
*   **Sword:** Physiotherapy AI. B2B only.
*   **ZOE/InsideTracker:** Biomarker tracking. Narrow scope (nutrition/metabolic only).

### Encrypted Vaults
*   **Apple Health:** On-device, but walled garden. No AI integration.
*   **Health Gorilla/Pebble:** B2B routing. No consumer app.
*   **Gap:** No consumer-facing "Encrypted AI Doctor" exists globally.

## 2. Brazilian Landscape (The Opportunity)

30%+ of Brazilians have hypertension. A personalized AI treatment planner for chronic disease is a massive market.

### Telemedicine + AI
| Company | What They Do | Privacy/Encryption | Gap |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Doctoralia** | Appointment booking | Standard | No AI treatment planning |
| **Zenklub** | Wellness + therapy | Standard | No medical treatment planning |
| **iClinic** | Electronic records (B2B) | Standard | No consumer app |
| **Unimed/Ame** | Teleconsultas | Standard | No longitudinal data |

### Key Brazilian Market Gaps
1.  **No E2EE personal health vault** — All Brazilian apps use standard cloud storage.
2.  **No AI treatment planner with privacy** — Apps are booking/therapy tools.
3.  **Low data ownership culture** — Brazilians don't track health data locally.
4.  **Fragmented healthcare system** — SUS + private insurance = no unified records.
5.  **High chronic disease burden** — Hypertension, diabetes, obesity = high demand.

## 3. How AI Doctor Differentiates

1.  **Privacy-First Architecture (Biggest Moat):** E2EE vault. User owns the key.
2.  **Longitudinal Memory Layer:** Structured graph of 500+ biomarkers over years.
3.  **Actionable Treatment Planning:** "Based on your HRV, reduce caffeine after 2pm. Evidence: Level 2 (RCT)."
4.  **Brazilian-Specific Edge:** Portuguese AI, SUS integration ready, localized for Brazilian guidelines.
5.  **Future-Proof Data Dividend:** Export as standardized `Health Data Passport` (FHIR/LOINC/SNOMED).
